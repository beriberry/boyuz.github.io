The Office: a zine

The perks of being a designer is I can make anything about anything I like,
and this project is dedicated to my favorite show--Scenese from The Office.

I used Bradbury Thompson's method, very old fashioned and colorful,
by using the combination of halftone lines/dots and CMYK overlays,
I created some odd but interesting illusions.
I enjoyed making this zine very much, hope you will like it too!
Watch The Office if you haven't.
