Who are we?

Cold Grillë is all about to bringing you a refreshingly original ice cream experience.

  • Using only the freshest ingredients, we provide you with a selection of innovative flavors and toppings—which have all been carefully chosen based on how their flavors meld and marry.
  • You can choose your long-time favorite, or something completely unexpected…or put a spin on your go-to order by pairing it with an eccentric topping.
  • But it doesn't stop there. Served to you in a way that looks just as creative as it tastes, Cold Grillë stands apart from your typical frozen dessert with a final product that looks almost too beautiful to eat…Almost.
  • Its preparation technique originating from Southeast Asia, Thai rolled ice cream is created using a liquid ice cream base, spatula, and chilled plate. Forget scoops with freezer burn…we chop, mix, spread, and roll your ice cream ready to order right in front of you.
  • So forget everything you thought you knew about ice cream trucks, and just roll with it.
