Alice Bliss: A Novel

Alice Bliss is a book about a little girl whose name is Alice and she is fifteen.
She's smart, funny, and clever. Not afraid to stand up for the things she believes in.
She also idolizes her father, and when he leaves home to fight a war she doesn't believe in,
Alice is distraught, she struggled a lot.
During the time he's away, she learns, and grows, and alway tries to be positive.

I used warm tone palette because they live in suburbs,
warm colors symbolize leaves, and nature.
When I read this book, just like Alice, I felt chaos in my chest, so much anxiety and depression,
So this is a piece to express my raw emotions using child like scribbles.
