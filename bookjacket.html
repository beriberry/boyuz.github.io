Alice Bliss: A Novel

Alice Bliss is a book about a little girl, and she is fifteen.
She's smart, funny, and clever.
Not afraid to stand up for the things she believes in.
She also idolizes her father, and when he leaves home to fight a war she doesn't believe in,
Alice is distraught.
During the time he's away, she learns, and grows.

I used warm tone palette because they live in suburbs,
warm colors symbolize leaves, and nature.
When I read this book I felt chaos in my chest, so much anxiety and depression,
it was in her chest too but she couldn't relieve any.
She struggles, but she also tries her best to see the light.
